Beautiful and always-sunny RIT (Rochester/Bearchester Institute of Technology) is well-known for high bear populations. The school often appears to be deserted, as all of its students are inside, playing World of Warcraft. There is an underground tunnel system for the inhabitants to avoid the surface world, with its evil and oppressive sun overlord.

Edit History

RIT was originally founded in 1305, by William Wallace. It was located in the middle of Rochester, Ireland, until it was relocated to Bearchester, NY. Recently, however, as a result of eminent domain, the school needed to be moved to a nearby area. The architect had recently watched Monty Python and the Holy Grail and decided that a swamp would be the best place. So ignoring popular opinion, he built it on top of a swamp. That college fell over and sank into the swamp, so he built another. That one burned down, fell over and then sank into the swamp. So he built another, and that’s the one that stands today. Most experts agree that the college will sink within several weeks of this writing.

Random "crazed gunmen" have been sighted on campus from time to time, accompanied by texts and emails to everyone you've ever looked at warning them of the situation. Look for one in your dorm this year!

Edit Layout

A map of RIT's campus. Clearly, RIT == BALLS.

For centuries, the only known map of RIT was the diagram seen to the right. Explorers were simply unable to obtain detailed information about the region, due to the wind, snow, and general swampiness. In 1885, Arnold Palmer, in search for the perfect ingredients for the now-popular Arnold Palmer beverage, braved the region. He found only bears. This was a poor era for the bears, as many were defeated and their berries stolen by Palmer. Unfortunately, the berries didn't taste very good. The only proof we have that this actually happened, is his journal, outlined above, and the detailed map to the left. Strangely, his final journal entry before leaving the region says only, "FUCK THIS PLACE."

The most detailed map of RIT currently available.

Little people did know that the bearstitute was originally designed and constructed as a sand-blasting facility to be located somewhere in Arizona. For how else will the Arizona ice tea company make their newest flavor? To humans, it provides a gentle breeze throughout summer and face-peeling gusts throughout the other three seasons. Although there is a tunnel network underground, it no longer unites both sides of campus. There is instead an above-ground “Quarter-Mile” which is used for traveling between "right" and "lefty". However, the "Quarter-Mile" was designed to be a moving platform, so it always slowly moves in the opposite direction. As a result, it causes the Quarter-Mile to actually be one Bear-Mile, or three regular miles.

Originally, the wind tunnels were not nearly as prevalent as they are today. From a joint effort between NASA and RIT, the entire campus was turned ninety degrees clockwise. In doing so, they created what is coloulolaoghqwally known as the infamous wind tunnels. Their effect on the bearstitute includes increasing heating bills and turning survivors into super-wind-resistant humanoids. It is clear from the journals found off of the dead that, "looking up is seen as defiant hubris, as well painful. Doing so often creates blinding from the glare off of the snow and razor wind tears flesh from what is now ice and used to be bone. Beside that, if one happens to be happy for any reason, smiling is frowned upon on the Quarter-Mile, and frowning on somebody smiling is smiled upon..." This etiquette is currently being studied by the Bearstitutes professors Smiley and Frownie Faces. The top men in their field!

Fun Fact: Oftentimes you can see the art majors flying because they carry their portfolios on their backs. It is considered sport to be able to hit them from a furlong.

Edit Gracie’s

A typical Gracie's meal

Originally a top-secret robot food production plant and bio-weapons research lab started by Alberto Simonevar-miguelle III, Gracie's was used to combat the rising Bear threat in newly settled Rochester. Alas, Rochester was overrun during the age of Bearquarius and all anti-bears were sought out and dealt with. Gracie's was discovered in plain sight, so Alberto Simonevar-miguelle III told everyone it was a cafeteria and named it after his illegitimate daughter Gracie. Gracie was named after her favorite childhood snack, which contained gracite. Gracite is the mineral added to all Gracie's food that gives it its unique texture and delicate flavor blend. It works for any food - raw chicken nuggets, over-cooked stir-fry - even cold pizza. It has the unfortunate side effect of Gracie, and once inside your body cannot be eliminated. It is common for one to say "You can't poop death" at a Bearchester funeral.

Gracie [nau-sea] — noun, plural graciepotomi [gggraaaaalph *splash*]
diarrhea-on-a-stick.

Gracie is a Rochesterianese word which, directly translated, means “flaming small intestine.” Gracie’s is a building in which a food-like substance (ph00d) is served to freshman, or 2nd year + RIT students that are too dumb to switch to an all-debit meal plan.

In order to make it at RIT, you have to be able to take “The Gracie’s Treatment”. “The Gracie’s Treatment” is a technique used to weed out the weak. In order to pass “The Gracie’s Treatment”, you must not die during your first year at RIT. Since freshmen are required to eat at Gracie’s, this proves to be a formidable challenge. There are two ways to pass “The Gracie’s Treatment” the easier way and the harder way. The easier way involves obtaining second year status by having 40 or more credits before the end of your first year at RIT. If you are a freshman with second year status, you no longer have to eat at Gracie’s, and you pass “The Gracie’s Treatment”. The harder way to pass involves eating at Gracie’s for the entire year, resulting in dangerous gracite build-up. Few have attempted this technique and retained enough of their sanity and internal organs to tell the tale.

Note: New technique has been exposed. Get a note from your doctor citing stomach pains and then talk with the health center. This trick has been verified to work. No button combinations or hex hacking necessary!

Note 2: A similar technique to the previous cheat. An allergy to nuts of any kind has been noted to enable easy escape of "The Gracie's Treatment" due to the severe cross-contamination of the various items on their menu.

Edit Species to Avoid

Although many of RIT's students are very intelligent and personable, there are several groups of people that should be avoided at all costs.

Edit Larpers

Located in the woods outside Gracie's, LARPers are often seen scaring other woodland students. They can be identified by a cloak (but are unlike #Cloakies) and screaming about spells, dungeons, dragons, many-sided dice, and their leet weapons. Similar to the GI JOES that also tend to siege the woods. It should be made clear that The Woods and Gazebo are strictly for pot smoking (see #Hippies).

Edit Hippies

The woods are not only home to the local wildlife, but a rather indiscernible number of students. These students are always hard at work making sure that the trails are clean and free of overgrowth. Be sure to say hi with bright flashlights and sirens during the late night raids when they are working their hardest. Note: most of these kids are found in SoFA(Note: No one actually goes outside everyone experiences the outside world via MMORPGS).

Edit Cloakies

This is an artist rendering of a Cloaky, in its natural tunnel habitat.

Cloakies are simply cloak-wearing brethren of the night, all day. Although few in number, their race is powerful and cloak-loving. They are most commonly seen at night, congregating in the darkest corners of the tunnels. It is suspected that they may be vampires, or purveyors of Dungeons and Dragons. It is nigh-impossible to capture their images on film, as they fear both sunlight and non-Cloak-wearing humans.

Edit Artists

Because RIT has a surprisingly good School of Arts and Crafts, artists can be found all over an otherwise very nerdy campus. They can be found smoking outside of Building 7, in their special dorm habitat where they can draw on the walls, or occupying valuable couch space in Java's. They are to be feared, because they actually have lives. They can be found in the labs late at night at the end of each quarter, having put their projects off until last second. Of special note are the Fine Arts majors, who hate all Design majors, Design majors, who hate Fine Art Majors, Photo majors, who hate everyone. And let's not forget the SOFA students (who love everyone), the New Media Design students (who love themselves), and the Craft Students (who love each other).

Edit Deafies

An immense number of deaf people, that spawn in the nearby swamp, congregate at RIT. In order to attempt to contain them, RIT created the Nazi Training Institute for Deafies (NTID) to function as a deaf-magnet so they would not interrupt the daily functioning of the university. Unfortunately, this has led to such people (colloquially "Deafies") to organize. Two consequences of this are the elevation of Honking as the official language of RIT, and of the election of a half-deaf student body president. Beware them, as they congregate in packs, and will use their honking in order to befuddle their prey shortly before devouring it, and leaving its remains strewn about all over where they dine.

It was thought that following graduation from NTID, Deafies would get jobs and move out of Rochester. In reality, NTID students work underneath the "tunnels" making RITZ Bits. We would be able to hear their screams if they could talk but...well...

There are certain species at the Bearchester Insitoot whose existences are under dispute.

Edit The Shameless (Seriouslyus Whatthefuckimus)

Last seen in the Wallace Library handing out business cards with his contact information to prospective girlfriends. No one knows where it comes from or where it lives. Many believe it is a nomadic beast that majestically patrols the countryside looking for 'the ladies'. It has been prophesied that if The Shameless ever found a 'ladies' that it would perform its most sacred ancient ritual, then proudly scamper off into the woods, much like a grue. The Bearchester Institute's Historical Society has created a dramatization of what this ancient ritual could be like.

Edit Deer Kid

At the beginning of time, when the first "Deafies" were rising from the swamp, something strange happened. A Deafie was left without a mate and its short 10-week life cycle was drawing to a close. In a desperate measure, the confused (and horny) creature ran into the woods honking incessantly. The elusive and immortal 'Deer Kid' resulted from the ensuing passion. Visible only at night, 'Deer Kid' emerges from the woods to terrorize helpless students and occasionally to bust some "fresh-ass" moves.

Edit The Squidicorn

The Squidicorn makes a public appearance for Squid Day, during finals week

This beautiful creature of the deep has stalked the waters of RIT for over a year now. Residing mostly in the lower levels of the Gleason residence hall, the Squidicorn, or Narsquid, as it has become known to locals, was rediscovered in late 2009 hidden beneath a broken table. The Squidicorn is our master. It manages our appointments. Only the Squidly one may decide when we visit the Dentist. Awrwrwrarwroawrooawrooarooo.
